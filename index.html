About the Artist

Sydney "Syn" DeFelix has had a background in art since a young age. Starting from private lessons to group courses and later on completing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 2024. Her tattooing experience started mid 2023 where she used her free time to practice the ropes and teach herself as much as she could. Later down the road she completed a proper apprenticeship and took up a artist title at that shop.

Syn's main style of practice is Otaku style tattoos, neo-traditional, semi-realism, and her own personal style most viewed in the "SPS Series".
